# Monetization Playbook - beaver-study-orchestrator

This file is a commercial planning artifact, not a guarantee of revenue. Use it to make the repository legible as a sellable product, pilot, workshop, license, or proof-of-value package.

## Commercial Lane

- **Lane:** B2C/B2B education productivity
- **Primary buyer:** Students, advisors, bootcamps, and education platforms.
- **Revenue wedge:** Syllabus-to-schedule planner with interpretable risk and what-if simulation.

## Offer Ladder

1. **Freemium planner** - pricing hypothesis: Free + $5/month student
2. **Advisor dashboard pilot** - pricing hypothesis: $199-$999/month cohort
3. **Bootcamp cohort-risk analytics** - pricing hypothesis: $3k-$12k platform pilot

## Acquisition Channels

- Student productivity content
- Advisor demo packs
- Bootcamp operator outreach

## Proof Gates Before Selling

- make test passes
- Risk drivers are interpretable
- ICS export path works

## Risk Boundaries

- Advisory guidance only
- Student data retention needs controls
- Avoid outcome guarantees

## 30/60/90 Day GTM

- **30 days:** Publish sample syllabus demos
- **60 days:** Create advisor report
- **90 days:** Pilot with one cohort

## Metrics That Matter

- Schedule completion
- Risk reduction
- Advisor review time saved

## Positioning Note

Lead with the specific operational pain, then show the runnable proof, then offer a narrow pilot. Do not pitch the repository as a vague app; pitch it as a concrete workflow with evidence, risk boundaries, and a next buying step.
